Dr Russell Keith-Magee is the founder of the BeeWare project. He joined
the Django core team in 2006, and for 5 years, was President of the
Django Software Foundation. He has been a member of the CPython core
team since May 2024.

Russell is a Principal Software Engineer in the Open Source group at
[Anaconda](https://www.anaconda.com/) [(a BeeWare Gold
sponsor)](/community/members/anaconda/).
